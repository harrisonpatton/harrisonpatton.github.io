About

Harry Belden was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. From a young age he has loved two things: performing and superheroes. Until age seven, Harry dressed as a different superhero every day of his life, until his teachers told him he had to comply with the “dress code” like all the other students. In order to find a socially acceptable way to dress up in costumes and pretend to be somebody else, he decided to pursue acting as a profession.

Proud Alum of the University of Illinois Acting Program
